Description

Diabetes Prediction web application to help users who have diabetes or who can develop diabetes. This is web application contains the following:

  • BMI Test to determine if the user is diabetes, pre-diabetes or non-diabetes. This will only work if the user falls under the BMI regulations to ensure accuracy.
  • Diabetes Prediction Test which runs using a machine learning model that can be trained even in production. This is also used for data extraction, if the user already knows their outcome then they could enter their results and it will be saved to the database plus the model will be trained on that new observation to prevent model drift.
  • Glucose Test to give users an outcome based on their glucose levels. This test uses the Fasting Plasma Glucose Test method to ensure accurate results.
  • Health Risk Survey to determine if a user is at low/high risk of developing diabetes based on their social determinants.
  • A dashboard for users to track their progress over time plus comparing them to other users that would hopefully motivate them to make a lifestyle change and improve their health.
